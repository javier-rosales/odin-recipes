Dorilocos

Description

Dorilocos is a mexican antojito (snack). It consists on Doritos topped with a crazy mix of flavors and textures: cueritos (pickled pork rinds), japanese nuts, cucumber, jicama, tamarind, lime juice, chamoy, Tajin and Mexican hot sauce.

Ingredients

  • A bag of Doritos
  • Cueritos (pickled pork rinds)
  • Cucumber
  • Jicama
  • Limes
  • Japanese nuts
  • Chamoy
  • Carrot
  • Hot sauce (salsa valentina or salsa botanera is a good choice)

Steps

  1. Lay your back of chips on a table label side up. Make one cut down the middle so it opens up like a bowl.
  2. Add the cucumbers, jicama, carrots, pork rinds and japanese nuts.
  3. Squeeze juice from the limes into the "bowl".
  4. Add as much chamoy and hot sauce as you like (adding a pinch of salt is never a bad option).
  5. Enjoy!

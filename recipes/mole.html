Mole

Description

Mole is a mexican food, when we talk about mole, we typically refer to the sauce, but it's always with meat, in this case, I talk about chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Chile ancho
  • Chile guajillo
  • Onion
  • Almonds
  • Chile pasilla
  • Chocolate
  • Tortillas de maíz
  • Pepper
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Cilantro

Steps

Note: Actually, you have another option to prepare mole itself, you can buy mole paste, it's because is a litte difficult to prepare the sauce, even in México is normal to buy this paste. So, the following steps consider buying the mole paste.

  1. Start by adding the mole paste to your pot to reconstitute.
  2. Pour in triple the amount of liquid, chicken broth, or water.
  3. Break up the paste
  4. Stri continually. The paste will start absorbing the liquid.
  5. Simmer for 20 minutes. Keep stirring. The mole will continue to absorb water. Mole should be on the thick side. It shouldn't be runny.
  6. Serve the mole with chiken.
  7. Enjoy!
